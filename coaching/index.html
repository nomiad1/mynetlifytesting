Career Coaching

You may have used a coach before…for work or sports.

Just as those individuals supported you in creating the best version of yourself, career coaching supports you in proactively creating the best career possible. An intentional career that maximizes your strengths, minimizes your weaknesses and/or non-preferred activities, and fills you with a sense of accomplishment and satisfaction.

Job search coaching can be incredibly helpful for clients who need guidance and direction in navigating today’s job search process. I coach clients through the steps to creating a targeted, proactive job search that is much more effective that the typical passive job search that is focused on job boards.

Conducting a targeted, proactive job search is not easy, however—you have to be willing to put in time and effort to achieve outstanding results. If you are satisfied with passively job searching and have little “free” time to invest in an active job search, then I don’t recommend you invest in a job search coaching program.

If, however, you are frustrated by the anemic results you’ve been getting from your current job search and would like to know more about the alternative, then please contact me and we’ll set up a quick call to see if my strategies are right for you.
