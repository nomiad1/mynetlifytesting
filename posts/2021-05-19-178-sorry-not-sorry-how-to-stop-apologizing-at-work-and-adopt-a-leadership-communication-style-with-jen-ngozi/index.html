Sorry Not Sorry! How to stop apologizing at work and adopt a leadership communication style (with Jen Ngozi)

My guest today is Jen Ngozi, founder of NetWerk®, a global women in leadership organization and movement on a mission to help everyday women worldwide build the community, confidence, and skills needed to become leaders.

Since their launch, Jen has grown NetWerk® from her basement to a global community of instructors, members and over 20,000 NetWerkHERS worldwide.

Jen has spoken at events with the United Nations, Vogue and Create & Cultivate. She has been seen in HuffPost, Weight Watchers, Thrive Global, ABC, NBC, Fox News and more.

In today’s episode, Jen provides the listeners with invaluable tips to STOP apologizing for everything and how to communicate like a leader.
