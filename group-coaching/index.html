ECC Group Coaching Program

Job Search Success 2019

Date & Time: 02/17/2019 through 03/31/2019

Are you ready find a new job that brings you greater joy, uses your skills and talents, and provides the kind of financial compensation you’re worth?

Here’s your all-in-one solution: Job Search Success 2019!

This coaching program is specifically for mid-career folks (at least 10 years out of college) who are planning to make a job change in 2019.

What results can you expect from this program?

  • Clarity around your ideal employer... you will identify who you want to work for and how to approach them
  • A comprehensive job search strategy that includes active and passive action steps... significantly increasing your chances for success
  • Well-honed interview skills so you can ace the interview
  • Clarity around your thoughts so there’s nothing between your ears getting in the way of your success
  • The support, instruction, and coaching of a job search expert and your fellow participants... you won’t be in this alone!

The centerpiece of Job Search Success 2019 is a six-week group coaching program. There will be 30 minutes of teaching and 30 minutes of Q&A and live coaching each week.

Here’s what we’ll cover:

Week 1: Begin with the end in mind (identifying your ideal employer)

Week 2: Getting strategic (identifying and ranking your target employers)

Week 3: Mapping out a multi-pronged job search strategy

Week 4: Your thoughts determine your results

Week 5: Interview Excellence (Part 1)

Week 6: Interview Excellence (Part 2)

But wait... there’s more! The program also includes:

  • Two additional bonus coaching calls – I’ll coach you on any career-related topic!
  • Materials and resources to support the teaching – yes, you’ll have homework!
  • Accountability partners – you have a partner to support you in your job search
  • Networking among participants – you’ll share leads, networking contacts, ideas
  • Closed FB group for communicating with other participants
  • Special offers for new or updated documents (see below)

I’m in! When does it start?

The program starts the week of February 17th and goes for six weeks through Sunday, March 31th.

Live calls will be on Sundays at 7:00 p.m. Eastern. All sessions will be recorded if you can’t make the live call.

What’s the cost, and how many can participate?

My goal was to make this program as affordable as possible. I’m limiting enrollment to a maximum of 12, so you’ll have plenty of opportunity to ask questions and get coached by me. Further, you’ll have the support of other group members for lead-sharing and accountability partnering.

Payment Options:

3 payments of $265 OR

If paid in full (by Friday February 15th ): $695 (save $100)

What’s this about special offers for new or updated documents?

With the purchase of this program, the following document discounts are available:

  • For updating resume/cover letter/ LinkedIn profile originally written by me: $75/hour (50% off regular price)
  • For new resume/cover letter/ LinkedIn profile written by me: $995 ($400 off regular price)
  • For new resume/cover letter/ LinkedIn profile written by one of my credentialed resume writers: $695 ($200 off regular price)
