INTERVIEW GUARANTEE

Exclusive Career Coaching is committed to your success. If you do not receive at least one bona fide interview within 60 days of receiving your finalized resume, we will rewrite your resume for FREE!

A bona fide interview is defined as being one for a position you have applied for (as opposed to unsolicited requests for interviews).

As with all guarantees, you, as the job seeker, must participate in the process and progress of your job search.

When you become a client of ECC, we will send you the Interview Guarantee.
