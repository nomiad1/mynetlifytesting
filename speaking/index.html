Here is a list of programs I offer:

Why Aren’t They More Like Me?

Leveraging Personality Differences in the Workplace

In this engaging and highly interactive presentation, you’ll develop techniques for communicating with, assigning tasks to, and scheduling the work of team members by taking into consideration their unique personality characteristics. This session, based on the MBTI personality inventory, will give you:

  • Keys to recognizing the personality preferences of team members
  • Techniques for “speaking the foreign language” of various personalities
  • How to maximize your team’s effectiveness using personality type

It's Not You. It's Your Brain

Minding Your Mind as a Primary Tool for Career Advancement

So many trainings focus on the doing part of managing your career...in this presentation, we will focus on the thinking part. The tool Lesa teaches has completely changed her life, and with regular use will change yours tool By gaining clarity around how your thoughts directly impact your results, you'll:

  • Cultivate a practice to become more aware of your current thoughts
  • Begin thinking thoughts that better serve you
  • Learn how to think to achieve any goal you set in life

What Did You Just Say?

Active Listening and Effective Communication in a Distracted World

Listening and communicating are skills that can - and should - be developed and honed throughout your career. With lots of activities and opportunities for feedback, this program teaches:

  • The steps to active listening and how to develop your listening "weak link"
  • The foundations of effective communication: clarity and conciseness
  • How to minimize distractions that impede listening and communicating
